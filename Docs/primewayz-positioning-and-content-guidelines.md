# Primewayz UK positioning and content guidelines

Internal reference for website and LinkedIn-ready messaging. Do not treat this as a public page.

## Primary positioning

Primewayz helps UK SMEs improve, connect and support the digital systems their businesses depend on.

## Short brand direction

Reliable digital systems and technical delivery for UK SMEs.

## Brand promise

Practical ownership from assessment and improvement through delivery and ongoing support.

## Buyer-facing positioning

We take ownership of the websites, CRM workflows, software applications and technical improvements that growing UK businesses depend on.

## Five working service areas

1. Website Visibility & Conversion
2. CRM & Workflow Automation
3. Software & Product Engineering
4. Managed Application & Website Support
5. Remote IT Team Extension

## Engagement-model distinction

- Services = what Primewayz delivers
- Engagement models = how the work is organised
- Plans = the capacity or level of support purchased

Common engagement language:

- Assessment
- Foundation Sprint
- Fixed-scope project
- Structured monthly delivery
- Managed support
- Dedicated technical capacity

A software development subscription is an engagement / delivery model for ongoing software and product engineering. It is not the company's main service category.

## Approved content themes

- Take ownership
- Improve existing systems
- Connect disconnected workflows
- Rescue stalled delivery
- Support inherited applications
- Reduce manual work
- Improve lead handling
- Maintain business-critical systems
- Structured monthly delivery
- Clear priorities
- Accountable technical support
- Reliable continuity
- Support beyond launch

## Language to use

- Business problems and outcomes first
- Systems being improved second
- Delivery approach third
- Technologies only where relevant
- British English

## Language to reduce

- Digital transformation
- Cutting-edge solutions
- Innovative technology
- End-to-end solutions
- Seamless services
- AI-powered solutions
- Cost-effective offshore resources
- Software development subscription as a primary category
- Generic claims with no supporting proof
